About Dev Leader
Nick Cosentino — known online as Dev Leader — is a Principal Software Engineering Manager at Microsoft, content creator, and co-founder of BrandGhost, an AI-powered social media management platform built for creators and founders.

Nick has spent over a decade working across software engineering roles at Microsoft, leading teams and shipping products at scale. Outside of his day job, he has built one of the most recognized C# and .NET education brands online.

His primary YouTube channel, Dev Leader, covers C#, .NET, software architecture, design patterns, dependency injection, performance optimization, and engineering career growth — all with a focus on making complex concepts approachable. Whether you are a junior developer learning the fundamentals or a senior engineer tackling distributed systems, Dev Leader content meets you where you are.

His second channel, Code Commute, is a long-form conversational show and companion podcast designed for developers on the go. Episodes explore real-world software engineering topics, industry trends, and career advice — the kind of conversations that happen between engineers, not in textbooks.

In addition to YouTube, Nick publishes a weekly newsletter on Substack and runs live coding streams on Twitch and Kick. His writing spans software architecture, C# language features, open source projects, and engineering management.

BrandGhost — the platform Nick co-founded — powers the content workflow behind many of the posts you see from Dev Leader. It is built for creators who want to stop manually juggling social media and start growing with AI-assisted scheduling, topic streams, and multi-platform publishing.

If you are looking to grow as a software engineer, follow the Dev Leader newsletter and YouTube channel. If you are a creator or founder looking to streamline your content, check out BrandGhost.
